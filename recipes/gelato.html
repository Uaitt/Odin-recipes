Gelato recipe

Hey! Fellow Odin Student, we have made the perfect lunch, the perfect dinner...now what do you think are we going to make?

Obviously a dessert! Today we are talking about the perfect Italian dessert...gelato (yes it's not called ice cream!)

Ingredients

  • 4 medium egg yolkls
  • 150g of granulated sugar
  • 350ml of milk
  • 250ml of heavy cream
  • vanilla extract (optional)

And now, as usual, time to cook!

Steps

  1. Whip the egg yolks with the sugar for 5 minutes, until you have a soft cream
  2. Meanwhile pour the milk in a saucepan and bring it almost to boil
  3. Transfer the egg and sugar cream to a saucepan, pour the milk slowly and mix! You can also add at this moment the vanilla extract
  4. Pour the mixture in a container and freeze it for 5 hours. Keep in mind that you have to break the frozen surface every 30 minutes
  5. After 5 hours, take it out of the freezer, break it again and let it thaw for 15 minutes
  6. You just made some fresh Italian gelato, congratulations!
