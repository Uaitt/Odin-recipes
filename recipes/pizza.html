A delicious pizza recipe

Description

Hello fellow Odin student! Today you are going to learn how to make a true Italian pizza, by an Italian itself!

Prepare yourself, and come with me!

Ingredients for 2 people

  • 300g of strong bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon of yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 100 ml of tomato sauce
  • Some fresh basil
  • 125g of mozzarella
  • Everything you want as topping!

Now it's time to finally go into the steps of this amazing recipe!

Steps

  1. Make the base
    1. Put the flour into a large bowl with the yeast and salt
    2. Pour 200 ml of water and olive oil together with a wooden spoon
    3. Knead for 5 minutes and let it rest
  2. Make the sauce
    1. Mix the pomodoro with the basil
    2. Leave it to stand
  3. Roll out the dough
    1. Split the dough into 2
    2. With the help of a rolling pin roll them out
  4. Top and bake
    1. Heat the oven to 250C
    2. Smooth the sauce over the basis of the pizza
    3. Scatter with mozzarella and olive oil
    4. Bake for 10 minutes until crisp

What you have now is a true, Italian and delicious pizza!
