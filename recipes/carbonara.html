The perfect carbonara

Here we are my friend! Did you like my pizza recipe? I know you have!

Why don't you come with me again today, and try to cook one of the best pastas lunch we have here in Italy! Your lordship The Carbonara!

Ingredients for 2 people

  • 100g of real guanciale romano
  • 50g of pecorino romano
  • 350g of spaghetti
  • 3 eggs
  • salt and pepper

Now let's dive into the recipe!

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil
  2. Chop the guanciale
  3. Beat the 3 large eggs togheter in a bowl with some pepper, then add pecorino
  4. Add some salt to the boiling water and add the spaghetti
  5. While the spaghetti is cooking start frying the guanciale
  6. Keep the heat under the guanciale low, when the pasta is ready put it on the frying pan with the guanciale
  7. Quickly pour the eggs and the pecorino
  8. Enjoy your lunch!
